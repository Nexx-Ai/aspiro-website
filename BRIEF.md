# Aspiro AI Website Rebuild

**Domain:** AspiroAI.digital  
**Company:** Aspiro AI Ltd  
**Contact:** enquiries@aspiroai.digital

## Strategic Direction
**Mission-led, consultative approach**
- Goal: Build trust → Book a call
- Not selling a product, offering partnership
- Audience: SME owners who know AI matters but don't know where to start

## Target Audiences (all three)
1. SME owner who's overwhelmed - heard AI is important but drowning in noise
2. Someone actively looking for AI automation help
3. Someone who's tried ChatGPT but wants something that "sticks" in their business

## Core Message
*"You know AI matters. You just don't know where to start."*

We're not selling tools. We're building minds that fit your business.

## Homepage Flow
1. **Hero:** Mission statement that validates their situation
2. **Problem:** Acknowledge the overwhelm, the noise, the failed attempts
3. **Our approach:** Human-first, consultative, not tool-pushing
4. **What working with us looks like:** Light services overview
   - AI Audit & Advisory
   - AI Automation Builds
   - AIPA (AI Personal Assistant)
   - Micro Apps
5. **CTA:** "Let's talk" → Calendly booking

## What NOT to include
- No pricing on site (qualify in call)
- No feature comparisons
- No enterprise jargon ("ML algorithms", "predictive analytics")
- No stock photos of robots or blue brains

## Brand Voice
- Consultative, human, grounded
- "Let's figure this out together"
- Warm but competent
- No corporate speak

## Tagline
*"Empowers Your Ambitions — not with tools, with a mind that lives"*

## Design
- Dark theme with purple accents (keep current vibe)
- Clean, modern, fast-loading
- Mobile-first
- Minimal animations - substance over flash

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 14 (App Router) or Astro
- Tailwind CSS
- Deployed to Vercel
- Calendly embed for booking

## Pages
1. **Home** - The full journey above
2. **About** - Steve's story, the vision
3. **Services** - Deeper dive on each offering
4. **AIPA** - For those who want to learn more about the product
5. **Contact** - Simple, Calendly + direct email

## Success Metrics
- Visitor → understands what Aspiro does in 10 seconds
- Knows if it's for them
- Clear, low-friction path to booking a call
